# Progression — Idle Raid RPG

## Level

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Minimum level | 1 |
| Maximum level (MVP) | 20 |
| Estimated time to level 20 | ~1 hour (test mode) |

---

## XP Curve

The curve is a gentle exponential — each level progressively requires more XP than the previous one, but the pace is calibrated to not block the player.

| Range | Estimated Time | Feel |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 → 5 | 10 to 15 minutes | Fast — natural tutorial |
| Level 5 → 10 | 15 to 20 minutes | Starts to feel progression |
| Level 10 → 15 | 15 to 20 minutes | Dungeon starts to make sense |
| Level 15 → 20 | 10 to 15 minutes | Raid unlocked, end content |

> Values adjusted empirically during testing.

---

## XP Sources

| Source | Amount |
|---|---|
| Defeat common enemy (expedition) | Low |
| Defeat common enemy (dungeon/raid) | Medium |
| Defeat Elite | High |
| Defeat Boss (dungeon) | Very high |
| Defeat Boss (raid) | Maximum |

---

## Level Unlocks

| Level | Unlock |
|---|---|
| 1 | Expedition Zone 1 (Forest) |
| 10 | Expedition Zone 2 (Ruins) |
| 10 | Dungeon available |
| 15 | Raid available |
| 18 | Expedition Zone 3 (Shadow Cavern) |

---

## Attribute Points

Each level the character receives points to distribute among attributes.

- The player distributes manually
- Allows customizing the build within the class
- Can be reset by spending gold (see economy.md)

> Number of points per level defined during balancing.

---

## Equipment Progression

Level progression goes hand in hand with equipment progression.

```
Low level   → Common items  → Expedition Zone 1
Mid level   → Rare items    → Dungeon + Expedition Zone 2
High level  → Epic items    → Raid + Expedition Zone 3
```

A level 20 player with Epic +3 equipment represents the power ceiling of the MVP.
